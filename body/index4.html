the color yellow

*disclaimer: this is a rough draft i wrote the day before presenting this*
Part one

Two raindrops racing down the window.
My mother brings me banana slices for my teethless self to gnaw on
To smear all over my face and chubby little fingers.


I see on tv the girls and boys walking home with their mommies and daddies hand in hand
As the sky drizzles its april elixir
They jump in puddles creating tsunami waves that their protectors are built for armoring
Yellow raincoat,
Colored rain boots.

It could've been my emo phase why I found black so captivating.
Or maybe the agony of middle school.
I remember my daddy told me his favorite color is green because his lust for money.
That tickled me, the colors ive always hated.
Green, Orange, Yellow.

Part Two

There is something about the contrast of light and dark that identifies as beautiful.
The way yellow wraps around our universal melanin skin is breathtaking.
Human forms bumblebees, black and yellow has never looked better.

Each summer, my family would always taunt me with how dark I'd gotten from being outdoors.
As if being a darker berry was a curse cast by social unacceptance.
sun rays combined with cocoa butter kisses gives me a glow like no other, and I love the juice.

Sunflowers are some of the tallest flowers that i've ever seen grow.
And they grow towards the sun, keeping their backs towards this pessimistic world.
Coming out of high school id aspire to be more like a sunflower.


Part Three

It is the sad truth that the moment after I opened my eyes, I open Snapchat to send my streaks.
Not a good morning to my family, or breakfast my nourish my body.
A newly filtered life I live, forgotten lively communication.

If you have tumblr, you’d know that smoking cigs is more than a metaphor, it's a fad, aesthetics.
It could be the coffee that stains teeth this situationally beautiful yellow color,
Or maybe it's the collapse, because we all know mental health doesn't care about hygiene.
Once my college professor advisor told me, “When life gives you lemons, make lemonade.”
Like all of our lives are designed to be a tall nice glass of chilling lemonade.
But I will take the lemon zest and make lemon scones to accompany a glass of tea.
Lemon poppy seed muffins, be the lemons on baked cod, or that homemade facemask.
My own speciality dish
